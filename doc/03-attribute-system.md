# Attribute System

Inspired by Persona-style stat mechanics, the real-life attributes are defined as follows:

## 1. Knowledge
Represents learning, intellectual development, and skill acquisition.
Examples:
- Reading
- Studying
- Completing online courses
- Working on technical skills

Purpose in App:
Supports daily learning habits, long-term mastery, and self-education consistency.

## 2. Charisma
Represents communication ability, confidence, and social presence.
Examples:
- Social interactions
- Presentations
- Meeting new people
- Practicing speaking skills

Purpose in App:
Encourages healthy social habits and self-presentation awareness.

## 3. Empathy
Represents emotional maturity, empathy, and personal resilience.
Examples:
- observing cues (emotional reading)
- adjusting tone 
- understanding othersâ€™ needs 
- anticipating reactions

Purpose in App:
This maps directly to Emotional Intelligence (EQ)

## 4. Execution Capability
Represents personal capability to plan, consistency and workthrough
Examples:
- Ability to complete tasks 
- Time management ability 
- Ability to break down goals into actions 
- Habit reliability score

Purpose in App: 
Understand character's Reliability and Productivity

## 5. Fitness
Represents physical health and body conditioning.
Examples:
- Gym workouts
- Running
- Stretching
- Sleep optimization

Each attribute will have:
- XP
- Level progression
- Milestones and unlocks
